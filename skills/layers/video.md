# Layer Skill: Video (`video`)

Use video layers only when the prompt explicitly calls for footage or a motion insert.

## Best For
- product footage
- background loops
- demo recordings
- cinematic inserts

## Output
Create an element with:
- `type: "video"`
- `properties.src`
- `properties.startFrom`
- `properties.videoDuration`
- transform and sizing properties

## Direction
- Treat video as a hero surface or background plane, not as decoration.
- Use restrained framing and avoid piling heavy transforms on top of moving footage.
- Most video reveals should use opacity, subtle blur, or a soft slide instead of extreme scale or rotation.
