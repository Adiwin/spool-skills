# Pattern Skill: Hero Reveal

Use for launch pages, product intros, and brand statements.

## Composition
- one dominant headline
- one supporting visual or card
- one subtle accent layer

## Motion Recipe
- headline: `textIn` by words or lines
- support visual: `grow_in_group` or restrained slide + fade
- accent: subtle delayed move or rotate

## Timing
- headline begins first
- hero visual overlaps slightly after
- accent lands last

## Tone
Clean, confident, and spacious. Never noisy.
