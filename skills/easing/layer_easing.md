# Easing Skill: Layer Motion (`easing`)

Choose easing based on tone, not habit.

## Preferred Mapping
- `ease-out`: default for entrances
- `ease-in`: default for exits
- `ease-in-out`: balanced transforms and calmer motion
- `back-out`: use sparingly for playful UI or social motion
- custom bezier: for premium, editorial, luxury, or cinematic control

## Professional Defaults
- Premium/product/UI: `ease-out` or a soft custom bezier
- Editorial/cinematic: custom bezier or restrained `ease-in-out`
- Playful/social: selective `back-out`, not everywhere

## Avoid
- bounce on serious prompts
- linear unless the motion is intentionally mechanical
- overshoot on large text blocks or luxury visuals
